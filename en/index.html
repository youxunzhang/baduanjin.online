What is Ba Duan Jin Qigong?

Ba Duan Jin, literally meaning "Eight Pieces of Brocade," is one of the most popular forms of Chinese Qigong and ancient Chinese exercise. Dating back over 1,000 years, this gentle exercise system combines slow, flowing movements with deep breathing and meditation.

Perfect for beginners, Ba Duan Jin requires no equipment and can be practiced anywhere. Each of the eight forms targets specific organs and energy meridians, promoting holistic health and natural wellness through TCM exercises.

  • ✓ Suitable for all fitness levels and ages
  • ✓ No equipment required - pure bodyweight exercises
  • ✓ Backed by scientific research
  • ✓ Practiced by millions worldwide
