What is Ba Duan Jin?

Ba Duan Jin, literally meaning "Eight Pieces of Brocade," is one of the most popular forms of Chinese Qigong. Dating back over 1,000 years, this gentle exercise system combines slow, flowing movements with deep breathing and meditation.

Perfect for beginners, Ba Duan Jin requires no equipment and can be practiced anywhere. Each of the eight forms targets specific organs and energy meridians, promoting holistic wellness and longevity.
